1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how AeroSIM Inc. (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Email address (required for all account types)
  • Full name (provided during email signup)
  • Authentication provider (Apple, Google, or email)

Purchase Information:

  • eSIM plan selections and order history
  • Payment method type (card brand, last 4 digits — full card details are processed and stored exclusively by Stripe)
  • AeroBucks balance and redemption history

Technical Information:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • IP address (collected by our backend infrastructure)

Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • Location data — we do not request or access your device location
  • Contacts, photos, camera, or microphone

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Account Management: To create and maintain your account, authenticate your identity, and provide personalized service.
  • eSIM Provisioning: To process your plan purchase, coordinate with network providers, and deliver your eSIM.
  • Payment Processing: To process transactions securely through Stripe and Apple Pay.
  • Customer Support: To respond to inquiries and resolve issues via email or in-app support.
  • Service Improvement: To maintain, protect, and improve the Service.

4. Third-Party Services

We share the minimum necessary information with the following service providers:

  • Supabase (United States) — Authentication and secure database hosting. Stores your account data with encryption at rest.
  • Stripe (United States) — Payment processing. PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant. AeroSIM never stores your full card number.
  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In — OAuth authentication only. We receive your email and name; no additional data is shared.
  • eSIM Network Providers — We share order details (plan selection, not personal data) with our eSIM fulfillment partners to provision your data plan.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for advertising, marketing, or any other purpose.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored on Supabase servers in the United States, encrypted at rest and in transit (TLS/SSL).
  • Authentication sessions are stored locally on your device.
  • All communication between the app and our backend uses HTTPS encryption.
  • Payment data is handled entirely by Stripe’s PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure.

  • We retain your account data for as long as your account is active.
  • When you delete your account, all personal data including your profile, purchase history, and AeroBucks balance are permanently deleted from our systems.
